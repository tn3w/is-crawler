# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, nationality, appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity.

## Standards

**Encouraged:**

- Welcoming, inclusive language
- Constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the project

**Not tolerated:**

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a [private security report](https://github.com/tn3w/is-crawler/security) or contacting the maintainer directly. Do **not** open a public issue for conduct violations.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
